About

Formal Bio

Vanakkam! I am currently Python Developer at UMM Digital working on a Review Management Platform, for Small and Medium Enterprises. I obtained my BE in Computer Science in 2018 from from Department of Computer Science at College of Engineering Guindy, where I worked on cutting edge research with Dr. A.P. Shanthi, and Dr. Bama Srinivasan.

My research interests include Computational Neuroscience and Systems, especially exploring new ways of Data Storage and Retrieval inspired by Hippocampus. In particular, my current research involves creating a Simulator to replicate the Data Storage and Retrieval that takes place in Hippocampus. [LINK]



Informal Bio

It’s often said that only the craziest people pursue careers in Neuroscience or Advanced Computer Science and here I am trying to do both.
